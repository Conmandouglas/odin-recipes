Eggs Recipe

Eggs are a relatively simple food any person, no matter their cooking skill level, can cook!

Eggs are full of protein, so if you are looking for a more healthier food, here you go! Get yourself ready, and we can start cooking!

Ingredients:

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 Teaspoon of Mayonnaise or Salad Dressing
  • 1 Teaspoon of Water (Optional)
  • 1 Teaspoon of Butter / Margarine
  • 1 Pinch of Salt and Pepper to Taste

Directions:

  1. Using a bowl, whisk together eggs, mayo, and water using a fork
  2. Pour in eggs into a frying pan, stir until they cook
  3. Remove the eggs from the pan and put on a plate when they are set
  4. Add the Salt and Pepper to the eggs (Optional)
